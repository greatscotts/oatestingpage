History

Since its humble beginnings, GLOBAL Innovative continues to Transform Lives through Innovative Education. It has stayed true to its core values of L.I.F.E.: Leadership, Innovation, Fellowship and Excellence. As it gains and grows in experience, GLOBAL Innovative has remained clear in its mission to educate the whole person through innovative, state-of-the-art, globally focused professional training. It continues its respectable passing rate on board exams, monitors and assists its alumni as it strives to deliver a great student experience. GLOBAL Innovative has introduced many innovations in its education programs and systems. These innovations brought its own share of challenges, which it has weathered through its positive forward -looking attitude. It constantly guides itself in sound principles and values, which stays true to God and country. GLOBAL Innovative believes that education is not only a leveling tool for poverty, it is through education that the hopes of our next generation and the progress of our country lies.
