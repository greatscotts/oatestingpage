    Executive Team

    The team behind the company



    Gian Javelona
    CEO

    Gian built the PUP Mobile Portal app, the first mobile service in the Philippines that enabled access of school services through a smartphone. Through this, he was able to start Orange Apps at the young age of 19. Among his recognitions are being awarded the Innovator of the Year by PUP, and being included in the Top 7 News Makers of 2012 by Manila Bulletin. Gian is deeply passionate about educational technology and is always finding avenues to inspire the youth to become entrepreneurs in the country.



    Justin Isiderio
    UI

    Justin Isiderio holds degrees in Multimedia Arts at Lyceum of the Philippines Manila.He was the Vice-President in the University Camera Org and focuses on 3d modeling, animation and Graphic designs. His Main focus is building products that has good user interface and modern design.



    Jason Castro
    UX

    Jason holds degrees in Computer Science and in Fine Arts majoring in Visual Communications. Among his achievements in college are: Best Thesis, Best in Systems Analysis and Design, Best in Web Design, and Programmer of the Year. He is driven by his life-long dream to build technologies that are well-crafted, elegant, and fun. He aspires to make a difference through his penchant for art and design.
