How it works





Learn with Ease

Simply download Khawna for free on your smartphone and learn on a variety of subjects—even while on-the-go—at your own pace and on your own time.





Earn Points

Collect points as you complete a course through Khawna. The more classes you take, the more points you earn.





Get Rewards

Use your earned points to avail paid courses and redeem prizes from Khawna’s rewards.





Khawna for Teachers

Be among Khawna’s pool of experts and teach proactive learners. Impact someone's life with your expertise and even earn along the way.
