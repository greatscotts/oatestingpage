Why teach on khawna?

Teach and earn

You do not need a teaching degree to be a mentor. Whether you are a software developer, investment banker, photographer, makeup artist, or anything else in between; be among Khawna’s pool of experts and teach proactive learners. Impact someone’s life with your knowledge and expertise and even earn along the way.

Scale your class globally

Education and learning does not need to be restricted within four walls. Khawna expands your audience to mentor students around the world.

Easy to use

Khawna’s tools for teaching aim to maximize the entire learning experience. With a user-friendly interface and integrated mobile services, Khawna makes it seamless for you to teach your class that enhances the total learning experience.

Our company developed Khawna to allow people to learn globally-relevant skills and knowledge via their mobile device. Our vision is to break the traditional approach to learning and make education convenient, fun, and easily accessible to everyone.
Help us make this vision a reality, Teach on khawna.
