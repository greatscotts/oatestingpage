What is khawna?

Khawna is a learning platform where you could learn the skills that are needed in the industry. In doing so, it bridges the gap of industry learning and makes education available to everyone around the world.The name “Khawna” is inspired by the Tagalog phrase ikaw na (you already), which is said in jest to a person who has achieved something great. We used “It starts with you,” as our slogan because we believe that “all of us can make a difference in the world.”
