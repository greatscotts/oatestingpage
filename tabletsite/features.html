Features


Administrator Accounts

This account enables the school administrators to update, post and monitor information and important news that they will be providing for their students.

 
 
 
 
 

Teacher Account

This account enables the teachers to upload and/or update their student’s grades. The grades uploaded can be access by the students and parents through Student Account and Parents Access, respectively. The website and the mobile app can update the information being uploaded in real time basis

Parents access

This solution will help to strengthen the transparency regarding grades, liabilities and other important school activities and announcements for the parents.

 
 
 
 

Student Accounts

This account enables the students to get the latest updates or announcements posted by the school. It also includes accessing their information such as student number, course description, class schedules, outstanding balances, other liabilities and real-time updated grades.
