How OrangeApps works within your school

 

Student/Parent enrolls to the school using their phone or the web

Through the advanced technologies such as smart phones and laptop computers Students/Parents can now enroll in your school and see the course details and requirements online.

 
 
 
 
 

Student/Parent can pay their school fees anywhere.

Through the hassle-free payment scheme, your students/parents will be able to save up in lining up at school’s cashier counters to pay their dues. They will be able to pay their school fees anywhere using our platform since we are partnered with different payment establishments such as banks, malls and payment centres.





School teachers or administrators can encode grades, post announcements and events online.

Updating the students with the upcoming and exciting school events/ announcements is just a click-away. Teachers will also be able to promptly manage student’s grades such as encoding, uploading or updating.

 
 
 

Student can access their school’s information, activities and announcement/s on any kind of device.

The students can give fastest updates to their parents by logging in to their student’s account which contains important information such as grades, pending dues/ school requirements and other liabilities. They can also access school’s announcements anytime and anywhere.
